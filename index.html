Frequently Asked Questions

Can I pair two AURA speakers in stereo?
Yes. Using the RISONIC app or your device's native settings, you can seamlessly pair two AURA speakers for a true, wide-stage stereo experience.
Can I use the speaker while it is charging?
Absolutely. The intelligent battery management system allows you to continuously play music while the device is plugged in via USB-C, without degrading battery health.
Does it support voice assistants?
Yes, the AURA features a built-in quad far-field microphone array that works seamlessly with your connected device's voice assistant (Siri, Google Assistant, or Alexa).
Is the RISONIC AURA waterproof?
It is rated IPX5, meaning it is protected against water jets and splashes. While it's perfect for the kitchen or a light drizzle outdoors, it should not be fully submerged.
What is included in the box?
Your package includes the RISONIC AURA speaker, a premium braided USB-C to USB-C charging cable, a quick start guide, and our minimalist carrying sleeve.
